# Introducing official tasks

The 2026 season starts with a challenge.

We've invited some of paragliding's major brands to design official tasks on Syrac.
Whether you’re chasing the top of the leaderboard or just flying for fun, these tasks are open to everyone.

Welcome to the manufacturers league, which will run until March 1st, 2027.
No entry fee. Everyone's invited.

## Ozone

**Where the fast kids play.** [Ozone](https://flyozone.com/) didn't mess around. Their two [Gourdon](https://app.syrac.org/task/7b2fe0d3-882d-43d3-a779-1ddffc5691af) [tasks](https://app.syrac.org/task/cc444183-4942-41e9-9735-5b3bbcc91d2a) run straight through their R&D testing grounds—the same place where they push their wings to the limit. Speed's the name of the game here. Can you keep up?

## Niviuk

**Versatility across borders.** [Niviuk](https://niviuk.com/) tests your adaptability across completely different landscapes. With two [alpine](https://app.syrac.org/task/24a4c05c-c45c-4a52-98af-0c3f8f2cf821) [tasks](https://app.syrac.org/task/9b860061-c0f0-46ca-8e94-92de566feec9) around their R&D office in Switzerland, and a third [one](https://app.syrac.org/task/53d20c70-8363-4750-a844-14a95e6378ee) near their Spanish office, they are delivering a diverse challenge that demands both technical skills and speed management.

{{< image src="https://syrac-media.s3.eu-central-003.backblazeb2.com/2026_manufacturer_league_niviuk.png" alt="Niviuk submission for the manufacturer's league" style="border: hidden" >}}

## Nervures

**One valley, two variants.** [Nervures](https://www.nervures.com/) joins the manufacturers league with a [winter](https://app.syrac.org/task/b3886354-e739-426e-b365-8e4216afc261) and a [summer](https://app.syrac.org/task/dfa7ef8b-4c45-4bcb-b3bd-9e14681fbf99) task near their workshop in the Hautes-Pyrénees.
